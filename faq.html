FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

For Businesses

Q1: What kind of companies do you work with?
We work with startups, mid-sized businesses, and enterprises across various sectors, especially those looking to scale their tech teams or need strategic recruitment support.
Q2: Do you offer customized hiring solutions?
Yes, all our hiring plans are tailored based on your industry, team size, hiring urgency, and budget. We offer everything from bulk hiring to niche technical recruitment.
Q3: How do you ensure the quality of candidates?
Every candidate goes through a multi-step screening process, including resume evaluation, technical assessments (if required), and pre-interview conversations to ensure the best fit.
Q4: Can I hire remote or offshore talent through MXA?
Absolutely. We specialize in remote and offshore hiring, giving you access to a wider talent pool.
Q5: How quickly can you help us fill positions?
Depending on the role, we typically start sharing quality profiles within 48–72 hours.

For Job Seekers

Q6: How can I apply for jobs through MXA Tech Consultancy?
You can submit your resume via our Careers Page or email it to [your email]. We’ll reach out when there’s a role that matches your profile.
Q7: Do you charge candidates any fees?
No, we never charge job seekers for any of our services.
Q8: Can I get help with resume building and interview preparation?
Yes, we offer resume polishing, career consultation, and interview preparation guidance — all free of charge.
Q9: I’m a fresher. Can MXA help me get a job?
Definitely. We support both freshers and experienced candidates by matching them with companies looking for enthusiastic, trainable talent.
Q10: Will I be updated after I apply for a job?
Yes, we ensure timely communication. You’ll be informed about the status of your application at every step.
